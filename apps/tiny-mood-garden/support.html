Tiny Mood Garden

Support

Tiny Mood Garden is a free local-only iPhone and iPad app for planting a small daily mood garden.

Help

If something is not working, confirm you are using iOS 17 or later, then open the app again and check Today, Garden, and Settings.

If reminders do not appear, check iOS Settings > Notifications and make sure notifications are allowed for Tiny Mood Garden.

Privacy

Tiny Mood Garden does not require an account, does not use a server, and does not track users. Mood entries stay on the device using local storage.
