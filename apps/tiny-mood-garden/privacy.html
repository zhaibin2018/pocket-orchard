Tiny Mood Garden

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

Data Collection

Tiny Mood Garden does not collect personal data.

The app does not require an account, does not use a server, does not include ads, and does not track users.

Local Data

Mood entries are stored locally on the user's device. A local mood entry contains the entry date, selected mood, created timestamp, and updated timestamp.

The app does not collect or store names, email addresses, location, photos, contacts, health data, notes, or identifiers.

Notifications

Daily reminders are optional. Notification permission is requested only if the user enables reminders in Settings.

Data Deletion

Users can remove all local data anytime from Settings.

Medical Disclaimer

Tiny Mood Garden is not a medical, therapy, or diagnostic tool. It does not diagnose, analyze, or treat any condition.

Contact

For support, visit Tiny Mood Garden Support.
