CleanScreen Drop

Support

Help for the tiny Mac menu bar app that hides desktop icons.

How to Use

Open CleanScreen Drop, then use the menu bar item labeled Desk to hide or show desktop icons.

Quit

Open the Desk menu and choose Quit CleanScreen Drop.

Uninstall

Quit the app, then delete CleanScreen Drop.app.

Known V1 Limitation

The menu bar entry is text-only and appears as Desk.
