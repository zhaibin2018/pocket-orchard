CleanScreen Drop

Privacy Policy

CleanScreen Drop is designed to be a tiny local Mac utility.

Data Collection

CleanScreen Drop does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.

Files

The app does not read, move, upload, delete, index, or scan files on your desktop. It only changes Finder's desktop icon visibility setting.

Network

The app has no account system, backend, analytics, ads, subscriptions, or network sync.

Local Settings

The app may store simple local state needed for first-run messaging or menu state. This stays on your Mac.
